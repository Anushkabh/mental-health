Our Vision

At the heart of BROKEN TO BRILLIANT is an online organization dedicated to helping women help and empower themselves. We provide evidence-based information and self-help tools for women from all strata of society to take the next steps in their mental health journey. We offer articles and insights into mental health topics, including specific mental disorders. Our vision is of improving the mental health of individuals and society at large, with a special focus on female mental health.

You can discover information about health and wellness topics as well, like healthy eating, exercise, sleep, work, and relationships. BROKEN TO BRILLIANT equips you with basic information to start living well now and to catapult you on your journey to recovery from mental health challenges.

The Mental Health Epidemic

In these isolating times, mental health issues have seen a marked rise in today's generation.

BROKEN TO BRILLIANT has a comprehensive strategy to lead patients from mental disorders to finding calm and stability. We primarily aim to educate people about mental health conditions and living well through information and resources, an extensive blog, webinars, podcasts, and more. Some common mental health issues plaguing women of today's day and age are:

  • Anxiety and Depression
  • Personality Disorders
  • Eating Disorders
  • Post Partum Depression
